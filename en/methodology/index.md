# Methodology

We adopt scientifically validated methods, combining traditional wisdom with modern technology to build a systematic training system.

## Training Framework

### Awakening Training System

Awakening Training is the core methodology of HumanLifeOS, divided into four levels:

1. **Foundation**: Breath awareness and body scan
2. **Perception**: Emotion recognition and cognitive insight
3. **Thinking**: Mental patterns and belief deconstruction
4. **Wisdom**: Transcending ego and unity experience

## Technical Means

### Modern Tools

- **Neural Feedback**: Monitoring physiological indicators through wearables
- **AI Assistance**: Intelligently analyzing training data, providing personalized suggestions
- **Community Support**: Online community interaction, growing together

## Practice Path

### Progressive Approach

1. Daily practice (10-30 minutes)
2. Deep training (weekend workshops)
3. Long-term practice (annual training camps)
