# Training

Systematic training courses to help you deeply practice Awakening Training.

## Course System

### Beginner Courses

- **Mindfulness Basics**: 7-day introduction course
- **Breath Awareness**: Focused breath practice
- **Body Scan**: Full body relaxation training

### Advanced Courses

- **Emotional Management**: Emotion recognition and regulation
- **Thinking Training**: Cognitive restructuring techniques
- **Stress Management**: Stress reduction strategies

### Advanced Courses

- **Deep Meditation**: Long-term meditation guidance
- **Mindful Leadership**: Leadership mindfulness training
- **Mindfulness Coaching**: Professional coaching certification

## Training Formats

### Online Courses

- Video instruction
- Live interaction
- Community discussion

### Offline Workshops

- Intensive training
- Instructor guidance
- Peer support

## Training Plan

### Weekly Schedule

- Monday-Friday: Daily practice (15 minutes/day)
- Weekends: Deep training (2 hours)
